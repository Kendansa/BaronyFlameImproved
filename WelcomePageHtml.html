The Midrealm is the third oldest kingdom in the Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA). Our territory consists of Ohio, Indiana, and Illinois, as well as parts of Michigan, Kentucky, Iowa, and a portion of the Canadian Province of Ontario.

Our neighboring kingdoms are:

  • Ealdormere (Western Ontario)
  • Aethelmearc (West Virginia, part of New York and Pennsylvania)
  • Atlantia (Maryland, Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina)
  • Meridies (Georgia, parts of Alabama, Tennessee, Florida, and Kentucky)
  • Gleann Abhann (Mississippi, parts of Alabama, Arkansas, and Tennessee)
  • Calontir (Missouri, Iowa, Nebraska, Kansas, parts of Arkansas)
  • Northshield (Wisconson, Minnesota, North Dakota, South Dakota, part of Mighigan, Mannitoba, east Ontario and west Saskatchewan)

Our current king and queen are Their Royal Majesties Ragnvaldr and Arabella, and our current prince and princess are Their Royal Highnesses Nikolai and Serena.
